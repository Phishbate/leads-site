Still have questions?

Why is it a monthly subscription?

These leads are researched each month, so they’re fresh and accurate (as opposed to part of a big mass list).

A monthly subscription allows you to set up your email campaigns once, then plug in the new leads each month, automating this acquisition channel.

What type of Shopify stores do you target?

We target Shopify stores that have a good amount of traffic (i.e. making money) but not so big that it’s hard to reach the decision makers. Our leads are category agnostic.  

Where are most of the stores located?

We primarily target USA based leads. 

Won't I be competing with others?

We will limit the number of people who have access to the list. We're talking low 10's. And our customers vary between agency types, apps, software etc.

E.g. a lead might recieve 20 extra outreach emails in a month from various service providers. This service is not about inundating prospects.

Is this for me? Who is this for?

This isn’t for everyone. Cold email outreach is highly effective if done right but it’s certainly a specific skillset. Ideally you or someone in your team has already run cold email campaigns or is confident in setting them up and have a great offer for new customers.
